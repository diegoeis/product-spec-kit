### Key Features

```
#### Support Multiple Payment Methods
- Cryptocurrency payments: allows users to pay using cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin, ETH and others
- Credit card integration via gateways: to facilitate integration and card payments on the platform without the need for official regulations.
- Support for digital wallets like Apple Pay and Google Pay: making it easier for users to adopt without needing to register new information on the platform.
- PIX payments: facilitating adoption with copy/paste code and QR Code payments;

#### Fraud Prevention
- Machine learning fraud detection: automating the detection of potentially fraudulent purchases and preventing money laundering as much as possible.
- Real-time risk assessment: with fallback to operations desk when automatic validation fails.

#### Platform Payments
- Balance payments: customers top up their platform balance to make payments directly on the platform
- Payment scheduling: users can schedule platform payments to be automatically executed on a specific day
- Open URL for third-party payment: customers can share the payment page with amounts to be paid for others to complete the payment.
```
